# Roadmap

This roadmap is intentionally practical. The goal is to make `wechat-literature-article` easier to trust, easier to try, and easier to contribute to.

## v0.2.0 - Demo and Trust

- Add a real open-access case study with source links.
- Include a sample article excerpt, figure explanation, and numeric audit trail.
- Add screenshots or rendered previews of Word-ready output.
- Add a "common failure modes" section with before/after corrections.

## v0.3.0 - Templates

- Add reusable article structure templates for:
  - ecology and climate papers;
  - biomedical papers;
  - AI and computational method papers;
  - review or perspective papers.
- Add title patterns for rigorous but attractive WeChat headlines.
- Add standard Chinese terminology examples for common research domains.

## v0.4.0 - QA Expansion

- Add a figure QA checklist with examples of good and bad crops.
- Add a Word layout QA checklist for common formatting failures.
- Add a table-to-prose conversion guide for oversized tables.
- Add a release checklist for public-account publication.

## Later Ideas

- Provide example outputs in both concise and specialist versions.
- Add multilingual support notes for English or bilingual public-account articles.
- Collect community examples based on open-access papers.
